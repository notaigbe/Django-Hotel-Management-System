About the Company

Establishment and Location

CANLEE INN Service Apartments was officially commissioned on 17th December 2022. Located in the serene, upscale Government Reservation Area (G.R.A.) of Benin City, the capital of Edo State of Nigeria, it is situated in a beautifully landscaped compound measuring over 1,200 square metres, which provides ample room for car parking, outdoor & indoor recreational facilities and our fully functional amenities; such as silenced power generating set to guarantee constant (24/7) power supply, professional round the clock security complemented by closed circuit security cameras (CCTV), clean potable water supply with in-house water purification system, free satellite TV & Wi-Fi services, etc.
